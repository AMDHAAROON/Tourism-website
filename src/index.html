Traveler's checklist

some things will be very important for travelling, so we need to check those by our checklist. Things to be remembered.

Passport. A passport is a vital document that verifies your identity and nationality,allowing you to travel internationally.
Travel Insurance. Protect yourself against medical emergencies and trip cancellations.Ensure coverage for lost luggage and other unexpected events.
Medications. Carry any prescription medications you need.Include a basic first aid kit with bandages and pain relievers.
Money and Cards. Bring a mix of cash and credit cards.Inform your bank of your travel plans to avoid issues with card usage abroad.
Comfortable Clothing. Pack versatile and comfortable clothing suitable for the climate.Dont forget a good pair of walking shoes.
Chargers. Bring essential electronics like your phone and camera.Include necessary chargers and adapters, and consider a portable power bank.
